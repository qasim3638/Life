### Business Overview

Tile Station LTD is a well-established tile and bathroom retailer operating across South East England. Founded in 2019, the company has grown from a single showroom to a multi-location retail operation with an annual turnover of approximately **£3 million**.

The company operates **four showroom locations** (with one opening soon), serving both trade professionals and retail customers. Our business model combines traditional showroom sales with a growing e-commerce platform, positioning us as a modern omni-channel retailer in the building materials sector.

### Mission Statement

To provide high-quality tiles, bathroom products, and expert advice to homeowners and trade professionals across South East England, delivering exceptional customer service and competitive pricing.

### Vision Statement

To become the leading independent tile and bathroom supplier in the South East, known for quality products, knowledgeable staff, and outstanding customer experience across all our showrooms and online platforms.

### Sponsorship Summary

Tile Station LTD is applying for a Sponsor Licence to recruit **up to 4 skilled workers** in key management and supervisory roles to support our continued growth and expansion. These roles are essential for:

- Managing our expanding warehouse operations
- Overseeing multi-site showroom sales teams
- Supporting the opening of our new Sydenham location
- Developing our e-commerce operations

### Justification for Sponsorship

#### Why These Roles Cannot Be Filled Locally

1. **Skills Shortage in Retail Management**
   - UK retail sector experiencing management skills gap
   - High turnover in retail management positions
   - Competition from larger retailers for experienced managers

2. **Specialist Industry Knowledge**
   - Tile and building materials industry requires specific product knowledge
   - Limited pool of candidates with combined retail/trade experience
   - Training period for industry knowledge is 6-12 months

3. **Recruitment Challenges Experienced**
   - Advertised locally with limited suitable applicants
   - High competition for warehouse management in Kent/London
   - Difficulty finding candidates with both management skills and industry experience

4. **Business Growth Requirements**
   - New Sydenham showroom requires experienced management
   - Current management stretched across multiple sites
   - E-commerce growth requires dedicated operational oversight

#### Recruitment Efforts Made

Prior to seeking sponsored workers, Tile Station LTD has:

- Advertised on Indeed, Reed, and industry job boards
- Used local recruitment agencies
- Promoted internally where possible
- Offered competitive salaries and benefits
- Provided training opportunities

Despite these efforts, we have been unable to recruit suitable candidates for these senior positions.
